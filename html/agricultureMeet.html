Agriculture Robot
Here are some Points on Agriculture Robot
1. Precision farming:-Agri-robots use sensors and GPS for data-driven decision-making in crop management.
2. Crop monitoring:-Robots analyze real-time data on crop health and conditions for early issue detection.
3. Autonomous navigation:-Equipped with advanced navigation systems, robots can work autonomously in the fields.
4. Weeding and pest control:_Robots can identify and remove weeds precisely, reducing herbicide usage.
5. Harvesting and planting:-Automation streamlines harvesting and planting processes, improving efficiency.
6. Environmental impact:-Agricultural robots minimize resource waste and reduce the environmental impact of farming.
